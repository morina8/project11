Welcome

Books, programs, and a quiet place to get something done.

Brookdale Public Library offers access to books, digital resources, study space, and community programs for visitors of all ages. Whether you are stopping in to borrow materials, use a computer, or find a quiet corner for an hour, the library is here to help.

Library Services

Visitors can browse the collection, use public computers, connect to free Wi-Fi, and take part in seasonal programs and events. Staff are available during open hours to assist with library cards, account questions, and locating materials.

  • Books, magazines, and audiobooks
  • Public computers and internet access
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the building
  • Study areas and quiet seating

Hours

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

Programs

The library hosts reading groups, workshops, and community events throughout the year. Program details are available at the front desk and by email.

Visitor Support

Need help finding a title, printing a document, or setting up a library card. Staff are available during open hours to assist.
