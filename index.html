Welcome

Books, programs, and a quiet place to get something done.

Brookdale Public Library offers access to books, digital resources, study areas, and community programs for visitors of all ages. Whether you are stopping in to borrow materials, use a computer, or spend an afternoon reading, the library provides a comfortable place to do it.

Library Services

Visitors can browse the collection, use public computers, connect to free Wi-Fi, and get help from staff during open hours. The library also offers spaces for reading, study, and everyday research.

  • Books, magazines, and audiobooks
  • Public computers with internet access
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the building
  • Study areas and quiet seating

Hours

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

Programs

The library hosts reading groups, workshops, and seasonal events for children, teens, and adults throughout the year.

Visitor Assistance

Staff are available during open hours to help with library cards, account questions, locating materials, and general inquiries.
