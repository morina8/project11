About

Providing access to books, information, and community resources.

Brookdale Public Library serves the community by offering books, digital materials, public access resources, and a welcoming environment for reading, research, and study. The library supports visitors of all ages with services that encourage learning, literacy, and lifelong curiosity.

Our Mission

The library is committed to promoting literacy, supporting education, and providing equal access to information. It offers a quiet and inclusive space where visitors can read, study, explore new topics, and take part in programs that serve the broader community.

Services

  • Borrow books, magazines, and audiobooks
  • Use public computers with internet access
  • Connect to free Wi-Fi throughout the library
  • Reserve study areas and workspaces
  • Attend reading programs, workshops, and community events

Brookdale Public Library welcomes students, families, and residents looking for access to materials, reliable information, and a comfortable place to read or work.
