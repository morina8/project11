Welcome

Books, space, and a place to sit without being bothered.

Brookdale Public Library offers access to books, public resources, and a quiet environment. If you’re looking for noise, you’re in the wrong place. If you’re looking to read, study, or get something done, this should work just fine.

What you’ll find

The library provides a straightforward space for reading, research, and general use. Materials are easy to access, staff are available when needed, and nothing is set up to slow you down or make things more complicated than they need to be.

  • Books, magazines, and audiobooks
  • Public computers and internet access
  • Free Wi-Fi throughout the building
  • Study areas and quiet seating

Hours

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

Staff are available during open hours for questions, assistance, or locating materials. Otherwise, you’re free to find a spot and keep to yourself, which most people seem to prefer.
