About

It’s a library. It shouldn’t be hard to use.

This isn’t trying to reinvent anything. It’s just a straightforward site that does what you expect it to do. You open it, you know where you are, and you can get where you need to go without digging around. That’s the whole idea.

What this is

A simple library layout. Nothing flashy, nothing trying too hard. Just a clean setup where you can find information, read a bit, or figure out what you need without the page getting in your way.

What you can do

  • Borrow books, magazines, and audiobooks
  • Use computers and free Wi-Fi
  • Reserve a quiet study space
  • Join small reading groups or events

Most of the work here isn’t what you see, it’s what you don’t notice. The pages stay consistent, the layout doesn’t shift around, and nothing forces you to stop and figure it out. If you can move through the site without thinking about it, then it’s doing exactly what it’s supposed to do.
