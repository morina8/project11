About

Providing access to information, resources, and community programs.

Brookdale Public Library offers a welcoming space for reading, research, and community engagement. The library supports residents of all ages with access to books, digital materials, and public resources.

Our Mission

The library is committed to promoting lifelong learning, supporting literacy, and providing free access to information. It serves as a quiet and inclusive environment where visitors can study, explore, and participate in community programs.

Services

  • Borrow books, magazines, and audiobooks
  • Access public computers with internet and printing
  • Use free Wi-Fi throughout the facility
  • Reserve study areas and workspaces
  • Attend community events, workshops, and reading programs

Brookdale Public Library is designed to be accessible and easy to use, helping visitors find materials, services, and information without difficulty.
