About

Providing access to information, learning, and community resources.

Brookdale Public Library serves the community by offering books, public resources, and programs that support reading, research, and lifelong learning. The library is intended to be a welcoming place for study, discovery, and everyday use.

Our Mission

The library is committed to promoting literacy, supporting education, and providing free access to information. Visitors can use the space to read, study, explore new topics, and participate in programs designed for children, teens, and adults.

Services

  • Borrow books, magazines, and audiobooks
  • Use public computers with internet access
  • Connect to free Wi-Fi throughout the library
  • Reserve study areas and workspaces
  • Attend workshops, reading groups, and community events

Brookdale Public Library welcomes visitors of all ages and aims to make materials and services easy to access, whether someone is borrowing a book, using a computer, or staying for a longer study session.
