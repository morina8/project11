About

Providing access to books, resources, and community programs.

Brookdale Public Library serves the local community by offering access to information, educational materials, and a quiet place for reading and study. The library supports individuals of all ages with resources that encourage learning and connection.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote literacy, support lifelong learning, and provide equal access to information. The library offers a welcoming and inclusive environment where visitors can study, explore new ideas, and participate in community programs.

Services

  • Borrow books, magazines, and audiobooks
  • Access public computers with internet and printing
  • Use free Wi-Fi throughout the facility
  • Reserve study areas and workspaces
  • Attend community events, workshops, and reading programs

The library is organized to help visitors easily locate materials and services, making it a practical and accessible space for both short visits and extended use.
